How it works

1

Create your Digital Human

Upload a face photo or short video clip. Set your identity type, confirm consent, and your AI avatar is ready.

2

Write or generate your script

Type exactly what you want your digital human to say — or give a topic and let the AI write a viral script for you.

3

AI generates voice + lip sync

The TTS worker generates natural speech. Wav2Lip animates your face to match every word with precise lip movements.

4

Captions + final render

Word-level captions are generated and burned in. FFmpeg renders the final 9:16 portrait video at 1080×1920, 30fps.

5

Download and publish

Your video is ready in minutes. Download it, post it to TikTok, Reels, or Shorts, or send it straight to ClipForge AI.
